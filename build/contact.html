About Us

Seed2Feed, an initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. We collect leftover food from events, hotels, and restaurants and redistribute it to those in need through partnerships with NGOs and school feeding programs.
Our mission is simple: ensure that no food goes to waste while helping people in need access nutritious meals. We focus on three key areas: Redistributing Surplus Food: Collecting and delivering excess food to communities.
School Feeding Programs: Providing meals to underprivileged students to support their education.
Sustainability & Education: Teaching communities to grow their own food and reduce waste.
Join us in making a difference—one plate at a time.
