Learn About Nutritious Food

Nutrition is key to healthy growth and development. Discover what makes food nutritious and how you can make better food choices.

  • Fruits and Vegetables: Packed with vitamins and minerals essential for a healthy body.
  • Whole Grains: Provide fiber and nutrients that support digestion and sustained energy.
  • Proteins: Help build and repair tissues, and are vital for muscle growth.
  • Healthy Fats: Good fats from sources like nuts and avocados help maintain cell function.
